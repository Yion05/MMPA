About The MPPPA

Advocating for Excellence in Private Physiotherapy

The Malaysia Private Physiotherapy Practice Association (MPPPA) is a professional body representing private sector physiotherapists across Malaysia. We are a dynamic network of clinicians committed to advancing private physiotherapy practice through collaboration, advocacy, and professional development. We advocate for clinical standards, business excellence, and fair representation in national healthcare policy.

Our Vision & Mission

Guiding Our Path Forward

Our Vision

To be the leading voice and support system for physiotherapists in private practice in Malaysia to the government, MPA, and related parties.

Our Mission

  • To represent private practices in various business groups & platforms.
  • To support professional development and business growth.
  • To foster collaboration, innovation, and best practices.

Association Highlights

Making an Impact

Press Conference for SST Exemption

On February 28th, 2024, MPPA, represented by President Ben Loo and DATO Dr. B.S. Bains, alongside representatives from the Malaysian Physiotherapy Association (MPA), held a crucial press conference. The agenda was to address the recently imposed Sales and Service Tax (SST) on physiotherapy services and to advocate for its exemption. This significant event, attended by prominent press companies, highlighted the industry's unified effort to ensure physiotherapy remains accessible and affordable for all Malaysians.
